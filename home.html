Hi, I'm Kim.

Who am I?

I'm an experienced design leader with 20+ years under my belt organizing chaos, directiong the ship, and creating compelling experiences that delight customers.

What do I do

I lead Product Design at Sycle, a software platform for managing audiology practices. I work in close collaboration with product and engineering counterparts to advocate for the voice and experience of users. I enjoy building systems and processes to foster growth within my team, and always strive to foster a collaborative culture with shared decision-making.

I've led the User Experience from concept to launch for many well known brands, agencies, and companies including Bitly, Martha Stewart, The New Yorker, and Nokia.

I also spent 5 years on the USA National Skeleton team.

When I'm not working

My design principles

  • Think in systems.

My management style

I believe empathy, trust, and transparency are the foundation upon which great teams are built. As a manager, I care deeply about the experience and success of my team. I strive to create an environment that fosters personal and professional growth by preaching what I practice, listening more, and continuously self-assessing.

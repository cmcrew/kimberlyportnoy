Why Basecamp?

Ever since reading Shape Up last year, I’ve wanted to work for Basecamp. I've never really used your product, so I’m not a diehard Basecamp, the product fan (yet). I am a big fan of Basecamp, the company. I don’t think there is one “right” process in product development, but I do know there are lots of bad ones. I love that you have a system, and that you’ve nailed it down so well you wrote a book on it!

I admire your straightforward approach to management, compensation, and communication. You seem to care just as much about how you work as you do about what you build, and I think that’s really cool. It's clear you know a thing or two about design. (My tired eyes appreciated the large font size you chose for your employee handbook as I was reading it late at night.) I think I could learn a lot from you.

I looked at your team and thought - “there’s not a lot of diversity here, I wonder if I should even apply.” But, you also called yourselves out on that lack of diversity, which shows integrity. That's the kind of company and people I want to work with.
